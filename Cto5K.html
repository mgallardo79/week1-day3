hold

The choice is your's but, when you pick you better pick wisely. You have many options in this department. Lately, I have gotten into trail running and I have to say that really like the Nike Pegasus Trail shoes.

  • Great foot response
  • Gore Tex water repelent
  • Well built

At the end of the day it is about personal preference and how you plan to log your miles, whether that is on the road or on the trail, like I mentioned choice wisely, as this is your biggest invest both in time and comfort.
